About Us

Two decades of dependable wholesale supply

Ashirbad Enterprise was founded in 2004 in Asansol with a simple mandate: give local pharmacies, nursing homes and diagnostic centres a supplier they never have to double-check. Two decades later, that's still the whole business — genuine stock, accurate counts, and orders that arrive when we say they will.
